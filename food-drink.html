Eat at Onvo!

At Onvo, we partner with some of the leading food and beverage brands to make sure you refuel while you fuel your vehicle. Our locations offer a wide variety of options, from quick bites and fresh coffee to hot meals for travelers on the go. With more than 20 quick-services restaurant locations across Northeast Pennsylvania, Central Pennsylvania, and Upstate New York, Onvo makes it easy to find something you’ll enjoy.

Food On the Fly

Onvo’s Food on the Fly program makes it easy to grab fresh, ready-to-eat meals without the wait. Our grab-and-go options include:

  • Fresh sandwiches and wrap
  • Salads and snacks
  • Hot breakfast, lunch, dinner items

Perfect for commuters, truck drivers, and travelers who want real food without slowing down.

Quick Service Restaurants

Want the satisfaction of a hot meal but don’t have time to take a break from driving? Onvo partners with Burger King®, Subway®, Tim Hortons®, and Popeye’s® to provide commuters, truck drivers, and travelers with easy access to consistent and delicious food options at reasonable prices. Stop by any of Onvo's 23 quick-service restaurants to fuel your flock on the go!

Best Coffee in Town

At Onvo, great coffee is part of the experience. Our bean-to-cup machines brew each cup fresh, delivering rich, flavorful coffee whenever you need it. Plus, we proudly serve high-quality coffee locally roasted right here in Pennsylvania!
