Our Story

Founded in 1988, Onvo (formerly known as Liberty), began as a family business rooted in Northeast Pennsylvania. Since opening our first location in Tunkhannock, PA, we've steadily expanded our footprint while staying true to what matters most: serving travelers with convenience, care, and consistency.

Over the years, our growth has included petroleum distribution, quick-service and full-service restaurants, and hotel properties; each designed to better support people on the road.
