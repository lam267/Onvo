Featured Impact

$75,000 Raised Annually for Breast Cancer Support

Each October, Onvo locations come together to support Breast Cancer Awareness Month through company-wide fundraising efforts, special promotions, team initiatives, and community events. Thanks to the generosity of our customers, team members, and partners, this effort continues to grow bringing hope, support, and critical resources to those affected by breast cancer.

Investing in Future Leaders with Educational Grants

Onvo proudly partners with the ExxonMobil Education Alliance Grant Program to support STEM education in local schools. Since 2007, the program has helped provide funding for classrooms, resources, and student opportunities across Northeastern Pennsylvania. At Onvo, supporting education means investing in stronger communities for years to come.
