Onvo Celebrates Grand Opening of TownePlace Suites by Marriott at Pottsville Travel Plaza

November 14, 2025

111-room, all-suite hotel expands Onvo’s hospitality footprint and brings new energy to Northeast Pennsylvania

Pottsville, PA (November 13, 2025) – Onvo, a Northeast Pennsylvania-based travel plaza chain, proudly celebrated the grand opening of the TownePlace Suites by Marriott in Pottsville with a ribbon-cutting ceremony and tour on November 13. Members of the Schuylkill Chamber of Commerce, Onvo leadership and local community partners joined to commemorate this milestone moment in Onvo’s continued expansion across Pennsylvania.

The 111-room, all-suite, extended-stay hotel offers guests a modern experience just off Exit 119 on I-81 – combining comfort, convenience, and local hospitality. Located in the heart of Highridge Industrial Park, the hotel provides easy access to major distribution centers as well as downtown Pottsville, the Yuengling Brewery tour, area wedding venues, and other regional attractions – making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure travelers.

“We’re excited to welcome guests to the TownePlace Suites in Pottsville and offer travelers a place that truly feels like home,” said Andy Aulakh, Chief Business Development Officer at Onvo. “As our seventh hotel, this property marks an important milestone in our mission to provide comfort, convenience, and hospitality for everyone we serve – from business travelers to families exploring Northeast Pennsylvania and beyond. We’re proud to invest in the Pottsville community and bring new jobs, new amenities, and new energy to this region.”

Each suite at the TownePlace Suites includes a full kitchen, high-speed internet, and pet-friendly accommodations, designed for comfort and flexibility. Guests can also enjoy a range of on-site amenities, including:

  • Complimentary breakfast buffet
  • Indoor swimming pool
  • 24-hour fitness center
  • 24-hour In-A-Pinch market
  • Outdoor patio with fire pit and gas grill
  • Highridge meeting room for corporate meetings, trainings, and private gatherings

“The Schuylkill Chamber of Commerce is very pleased to celebrate the 111-room, extended-stay TownePlace Suites by Marriott, the investment by Onvo and what it represents to Schuylkill County including commerce, economic development and tourism,” said Robert S. Carl Jr. President and CEO at the Schuylkill Chamber of Commerce. “This location will continue to support both business growth, leisure travel and tourism to Schuylkill County. This investment by Onvo complimenting the Onvo Travel

Plaza location is a significant hotel and amenity addition to the Schuylkill County region encouraging more business leaders and guests to visit the region. We are grateful for Onvo’s investment in Schuylkill County.”

Onvo’s investment in the community was also displayed through a couple of donation announcements. Onvo presented a check for $2,500 to the local AMVETS to support their Gordon Memorial Park project. They donated an additional $2,500 to support Cass Township.

The hotel which will create 30 additional jobs and is part of a larger development by the company which includes a flagship Onvo travel plaza, which opened in 2024. The travel plaza features a modern convenience store, Burger King, showers, laundry facilities, Onvo’s “Food on the Fly” made-to-order menu, handcrafted coffee program, and the area’s first Barking Lot dog park. Together, the plaza and hotel create a one-stop travel destination where visitors can fuel up, refresh, and rest – all in one convenient location.

Guests can now book rooms at the TownePlace Suites at https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/mdtpt-towneplace-suites-pottsville/overview/ .

About Onvo

Founded in 1988, Onvo is a family business with its roots in Northeast Pennsylvania. The Onvo story begins 35 years ago with the first truck stop just outside of Scranton, PA. Since then, the Onvo team has built a network of travel plazas, convenience stores, and restaurants dedicated to serving the travelers of Pennsylvania, New York, and beyond. As of 2025, the Onvo family of businesses now includes 40 travel plazas and gas stations, 24 quick-service and full-service restaurants, petroleum distribution, and 7 hotels in Pennsylvania and Upstate New York.
